Builders of the Sloan Digital Sky Survey

The Builders of the SDSS are those individuals whose contributions to project infrastructure make the exciting SDSS science possible. Specifically, these are the individuals who have contributed two years or more of effort to the infrastructure of the project writing pipeline software, building hardware, or through leadership and fundraising. The list will grow with time.
