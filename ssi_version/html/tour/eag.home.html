Mission

The mission of the Experimental Astrophysics Group is to conduct original research in astrophysics. This is accomplished partly through the direct efforts of the staff scientists in the EAG, partly by providing the linkage to collaborative efforts either within Fermilab or with outside institutions, and partly by providing a home base to attract new projects in experimental astrophysics to Fermilab.

Goals

The broad goals are:
1) provide a productive working environment for astronomical data collection and analysis;
2) provide scientific expertise that complements and amplifies that of the Theoretical Astrophysics Group and other physics efforts at Fermilab;
3) provide the scientific liaison that enables Fermilab to participate in multi-institutional projects. The specific goals for the next few years center on an outstanding institutional commitment to the Sloan Digital Sky Survey, namely to provide and maintain the data acquisition and processing systems. The EAG works closely with the Online Systems/Online Projects Group in this matter, and has also brought to bear expertise from elsewhere in the Laboratory.
