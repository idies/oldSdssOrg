SDSS Collaboration Meeting at Fermilab

March 30 - April 2, 2001


This meeting aims to summarize the status of the SDSS project with particular emphasis on the imminent early data release. Tutorial sessions on the use of SX will be available. Working group sessions will give the opportunity to present exciting current results, discuss scientific and technical problems, and share useful ideas for future studies. There will also be opportunities to contribute shorter talks.
