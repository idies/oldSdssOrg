SDSS Collaboration Meeting at Fermilab

March 30 - April 2, 2001

Registration Form



The deadline for registration is March 15, 2001. A registration fee is $25 for students, $75 for others, and is payable by check (made out to Fermilab) upon your arrival. For additional information about accommodations and meeting arrangements, please contact Luann O'Boyle (luann@fnal.gov).

Note: You are responsible for making your own hotel reservations.
         We have arranged for a special rate of $89 (plus tax) at the Hilton hotel (630-505-0900).
         This is only available if you book the hotel room before March 1st.

One of the more exciting aspects of this meeting will be a series of tutorial sessions on SX usage . These will be carried out in parallel to the working group sessions. In addition, there will be a "machine gun science" session for short talks (5 mins). We encourage everyone to sign up for one of the sessions.
