2.5m Telescope and Instruments

 

The telescope and its somewhat unusual optical design waddell98 are described in detail in york00 and the SDSS Project Book, available on the web. The camera and the pair of double spectrographs are discussed there as well; see also gunn98. We review here only details which are of direct relevance to working with the data.
