Combined Spectra

File Format: FITS image

Name: spMerge2d-YYYY-ZZZZZ-AA-FFF.fit, where YYYY is the plate ID, ZZZZZ is the MJD the plate was mapped, AA is the unique mapping run number for that plate on that MJD, and FFF is the fiber id.

Produced by: spectro2d

Used by: spectro1d

Archived? Yes. Tracked file.

Description

The combined spectrum over all exposures (potentially spanning multiple sequences) for a given fiber of a given mapped plate.

Primary Header

Contains the flux in the first row, and the estimated standard deviation in the flux in the second, both binned log-linear, in units of 10^-17 ers/s/cm^2/A. The length of the spectra (NAXIS1) can vary, depending on the pixel binning and wavelength coverage.
