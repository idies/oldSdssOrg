Image of the Week

Sextans B is a relatively nearby irregular galaxy just beyond the Local Group, which contains our Milky Way Galaxy. The galaxy is close enough that the brightest blue and red stars are easily visible, along with numerous star clusters. (Caption by Stephen Kent and Mark Schaffer).
