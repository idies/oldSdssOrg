The Japan Participation Group is a group of astronomers from several universities and the National Astronomical Observatory. Together with American collaborators, JPG had heavy involvement in constructing the mosaic CCD photometric camera, playing a major part in the fabrication and test of optical filters, electronics and mechanical parts. JPG purchased most of the CCDs for the project. Scientists from JPG are also engaged in providing simulations and verification of the photometric pipeline and play a major role in establishing and maintaining the photometric reference systems for the Sky Survey.
