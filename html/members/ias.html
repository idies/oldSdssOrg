A founding partner of the Sloan Digital Sky Survey, the Institute for Advanced Study has helped to define the science goals of the project and is currently involved with the quasar science produced by the Survey. Institute faculty and members serve on the Science Advisory Council and have made contributions in such areas as target selection, simulation of survey data, large-scale structure, databases and other complex technical problems.
