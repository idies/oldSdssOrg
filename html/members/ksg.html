The Korean Scientist Group (KSG) consists of astronomers at Korea Institute for Advanced Study and several universities in Korea. KSG has joined the SDSS as an Associate member in July, 2004. The KSG members are interested in information in large-scale structure and quasars, gravitational lensing, galaxy properties, and Galactic structure and kinematics.
