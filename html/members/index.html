Participating Institutions

The SDSS-II Collaboration includes 25 Participating Institutions and several External Participants at other institutions. Some of the Participating Institutions are vested in all three of the SDSS-II projects --- Legacy, SEGUE, and Supernovae, while some are listed in only one or two of these projects.

The Advisory Council represents the Participating Institutions and advises the ARC Board of Governors on matters relating to SDSS-II. The Collaboration Council helps manage collaboration affairs, including publications, meetings, and external collaborations.
