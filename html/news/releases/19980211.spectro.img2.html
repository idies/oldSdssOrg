Plug Plate Array

Astrophysicist Rich Kron, of the University of Chicago and Fermilab, inserts optical fibers into a pre-drilled "plug-plate," part of the Sloan Digital Sky Survey's unique spectrographic system for determining the distances to stars, galaxies and quasars.
