The data reported here for imaging and spectroscopy concern the Northern Survey (North Galactic Cap), plus the three stripes in the Southern Galactic Cap (equator and the two outrigger stripes). Together these are the primary SDSS survey, and we measure progress with respect to a baseline goal (column headed "Base"). Additional data are also being obtained (namely repeated scans of the equator in the South, and special plates in the South), but they fall outside the above reporting categories.
