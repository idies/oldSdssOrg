Operational Readiness Review
July 14-15, 1999

Logistics for the Review

The controls and interlocks review will be held at Fermilab.  Directions for getting to Fermilab can be found at the Fermilab Visitors Information web site.   The review will take place in Curia II, which is a meeting room on the second floor of Wilson Hall.  Wilson Hall is the 16-story hi-rise building at Fermilab.  Since it's the only hi-rise on the site, you won't be able to miss it.   To reach Curia II, enter Wilson Hall through the main doors and walk through the atrium to the stairs at the south end of the cafeteria.  Go up the stairs to the second floor and turn right (east).  Curia II is located in the southeast corner of the building. 

For our out-of-town guests, there are many hotels near Fermilab.  A list of these can be found at the Fermilab accommodations website.  Many offer special rates for Fermilab visitors, so please be sure to ask for these when making reservations.  As a point of reference, all of the reviewers will be staying at the Holiday Inn in Naperville. 

We will start the reviews promptly at 8:30 am each day and will finish by 4:00 pm on Thursday, July 15.  In making your travel arrangements out of Chicago, allow at least two hours for the drive from Fermilab to the airport (either O'Hare or Midway).   Chicago rush-hour traffic can be brutal.

If you have any special needs or would like additional assistance with directions or your travel plans, please do not hesitate to contact Lory Curry at 630-840-2568 or Bill Boroski at 630-840-4344.
