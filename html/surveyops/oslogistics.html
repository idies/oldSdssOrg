Review of Observing Systems and Survey Operations

Review Logistics

The Observing Systems and Survey Operations review will be held at Apache Point Observatory on April 25-27, 1999.  Directions for getting to Apache Point can be found at the Apache Point Visitor's Information web site.

The review will take place in the meeting room at the National Solar Observatory Visitor Center.  The Visitor Center is located next to APO's site in Sunspot, NM.  To reach the Visitor Center, continue past the entrance to Apache Point on Hwy 6563 and enter the National Solar Observatory facility. At the stop sign, turn left and follow the signs to the Visitor Center. You may also walk to the Visitor Center from Apache Point using Forest Trail 1050; please ask site staff for information.

For our out-of-town guests, on-site as well as off-site accommodations will be assigned by the APO staff.  A separate e-mail providing details will be sent to review participants.  Be sure to submit an APO housing reservation if you plan to attend the review. As a point of reference, only reviewers will be staying on-site at Apache Point.  Other review participants will be housed near the observatory.

Housing for review participants will be provided at no cost.  If you wish not to take advantage of APO housing, additional accommodations can be found in Cloudcroft and Alamogordo or surrounding communities.

In making your travel arrangements to Apache Point, allow at least two hours for the drive from El Paso and 4-5 hrs. for the drive from Albuquerque.

If you have any special housing needs or questions, please contact Gretchen Van Doren at 505-437-6822.
