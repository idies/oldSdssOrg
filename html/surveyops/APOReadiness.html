The readiness review will be held at Apache Point Observatory; Directions for getting to Apache Point can be found at the Apache Point Visitor's Information web site.

The review will take place at the National Solar Observatory Visitor Center meeting room located next to APO's site in Sunspot, NM; to reach the Visitor Center, continue past the entrance to Apache Point on Hwy 6563 and enter the National Solar Observatory facility. At the stop sign, turn left and follow the signs to the Visitor Center. you may also walk to the Visitor Center from Apache Point using Forest Trail 1050; please ask site staff for information.

For our out-of-town guests, onsite as well as offiste accommodations will be assigned. A separate e-mail will be issued providing details. Be sure to submit an APO housing reservation if you plan to attend. As a point of reference, only reviewers will be staying onsite at Apache Point. 

In making your travel arrangements to Apache Point, allow at least two hours for the drive from El Paso and 4-5 hrs. for the drive from Albuquerque.

If you have any special housing needs or questions, please contact Gretchen Van Doren at 505-437-6822.
