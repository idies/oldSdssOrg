Controlled Documents

The following documents define the high level requirements and specifications for the SDSS.  All changes must be approved by the SDSS Change Control Board.  Requests or suggestions for changes must be made following the SDSS change control process defined above.

Science

Top Level Science Requirements

This document defines the requirements for image quality, photometric and astrometric calibrations, target selection, spectroscopy, and operations. Contained in the document is also a summary list of specifications of the telescope and instrument that affect the science requirements. This document is maintained by Michael Strauss.
Revision Log (collaborators only)

Telescope

2.5-Meter Telescope Requirements (collaborators only)

This document defines the performance requirements for the telescope mechanical systems.  This includes the motion control system, interlocks. Placeholders are included for requirements on optics and instruments, including the fiber systems.  There is also a section on telescope and enclosure parameters relevant to telescope performance issues.  This document is maintained by Bill Boroski.
Revision Log (collaborators only)

Software

Software Requirements (collaborators only)

This document defines the requirements for performance and deliverables from the software.  Top-level science requirements are also here but for the most part are superseded by the Science Requirements document above.  This document is maintained by Steve Kent.
Revision Log (collaborators only)

Data Flow

SDSS Data Flow

This document defines the flow of data through survey operations, from the point of acquiring data from the sky to the point where data is made available to the SDSS Collaboration and the astronomy community.  This document is maintained by Bill Boroski.
