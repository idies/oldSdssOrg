TR-99040
SDSS Trip Report

Manipulator and Instrument Lift Work
Apr 2-5, 1999

Carl Lindenmeyer
Fermi National Accelerator Laboratory
Batavia, IL 60510 USA

The following items were addressed on our last trip to APO:

  • Mounted new operator handles on inside and outside Manipulators. The new handles are pultruded Polyester Fiberglass with the grip switch on the bottom.

  • Mounted longer fork tines on the outdoor Manipulator.

  • Mounted accumulator on Instrument Lift. We were unable to bleed the system because the telescope could not be exposed. I have explained to French the bleeding procedure, a 5 Min. job.

  • We were unable to replace the rack on the outdoor Manipulator due to weather conditions Sunday morning. This is low priority and can be done later. The new rack is stored out of the way in the support building.

  • The air leaks in the interior manipulator had already been fixed by APO staff.
