Approved SDSS Projects Involving External Collaborators
The Policy on External Collaborators has been in effect since it was announced to the SDSS Collaboration on 4/22/99. "Post Date" below indicates the date a proposed project was posted to sdss-general, the general mailing list for the Survey collaboration, as well as to the Chair of the SDSS Collaboration Council.

For completed projects involving external collaborators, see completed_ext_coll.html.
